This holiday season, Digitas is making a donation to One Warm Coat, a national nonprofit that works to provide free, warm coats and clothing to any person in need.

Health experts report that even a 2-degree drop in body temperature results in reduced heart rate, loss of coordination, and confusion. For most, warm coats and clothing solve the problem. But for nearly 15% of Americans living in poverty, warm winter clothing is a budget “extra.”

Join Digitas in donating to One Warm Coat, and help those in need stay warm this winter.
